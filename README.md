# AI-Powered Tailwind Layout Renderer
Write prompt, click Send, take component and copy/paste to your project. Simple as is :p

For using this you need to geterate API-key on https://platform.openai.com/. Then add your API-key from to settings on site and adjust assistant instructions which will be sent with your prompt every time.

**NOTE:**  We DO NOT store your API-key on our end. API-key stored in your browser `sessionStorage` until your browser closed.
